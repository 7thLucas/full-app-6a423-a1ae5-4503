# SpeakUp — AI English Speaking Tutor

## Product Identity
- **App Name**: SpeakUp
- **Tagline**: Your AI English tutor — patient, personalized, always on
- **Category**: EdTech / Language Learning SaaS
- **Brand Tone**: Encouraging, patient, modern, friendly, confidence-building

## Users & Audience
- **Primary Users**: Adult English learners (non-native speakers), ages 18–45
- **Proficiency Tiers**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
- **Learning Goals**: Daily conversation, business English, travel, job interviews, presentations, exam preparation
- **Core Pain Point**: No accessible, judgment-free space to practice speaking English and receive real-time personalized feedback without expensive human tutors or rigid classroom schedules

## Core Value Proposition
SpeakUp gives every learner a private AI English tutor available 24/7. Through voice-first conversations, it delivers instant feedback on pronunciation, grammar, vocabulary, and fluency — and adapts lessons to each learner's level and goals, creating a confidence-building experience comparable to having a personal language coach.

## Onboarding Flow
1. Proficiency assessment (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
2. Learning goals selection (daily conversation, business, travel, interviews, presentations, exams)
3. Improvement areas selection
4. Personalized learning plan generated by AI

## Key Features

### 1. AI Voice Conversation Tutor
Natural spoken conversations with an AI that holds back corrections until after each turn, maintaining conversation flow while providing feedback.

### 2. Personalized Learning Path
AI-generated learning plan from proficiency assessment. Plan continuously adapts based on user progress and recurring mistakes.

### 3. Speaking Practice Scenarios
Real-life simulations: ordering food, traveling, business meetings, job interviews, presentations, and everyday social situations.

### 4. Pronunciation Feedback
Real-time pronunciation scoring after each voice turn. Targeted tips for specific sounds and patterns. Non-interruptive delivery.

### 5. Grammar & Vocabulary Coaching
Plain-language grammar explanations. Contextual vocabulary introduction mid-conversation. Suggestions for more natural, fluent phrasing.

### 6. Conversation History
Review past sessions with full AI feedback, corrections, and vocabulary learned captured per session.

### 7. Daily Practice Challenges
Short daily speaking exercises designed to build consistent habit. Tied to streak tracking.

### 8. Progress Tracking Dashboard
- Daily speaking goal completion
- Current proficiency level
- Speaking streak
- Weekly learning progress
- Pronunciation score
- Grammar improvement score
- Vocabulary growth count
- Recently learned words
- Upcoming practice recommendations
- AI insights and personalized tips

### 9. Achievement Badges
Milestone rewards for streaks, vocabulary milestones, scenario completions, and level-ups.

### 10. AI Coach Notifications
Proactive personalized reminders:
- Daily speaking practice reminders
- Weekly learning summaries
- Vocabulary review reminders
- Pronunciation practice suggestions
- Progress milestones and achievement alerts
- Personalized exercises based on past mistakes
- Motivation messages after inactivity periods

User controls: enable/disable each notification type; set frequency preferences.

## AI Tutor Behavior
- Holds natural voice conversations in English
- Corrects pronunciation post-turn (non-interruptive)
- Explains grammar mistakes in simple, accessible language
- Suggests more natural or fluent ways to express ideas
- Introduces contextually relevant vocabulary during conversation
- Dynamically adjusts difficulty to current user level
- Answers English questions at any time
- Uses positive reinforcement to build speaking confidence

## Design Principles
- Voice-first: speaking practice is the primary interaction mode
- Adaptive: difficulty and content adjust to the learner in real time
- Consistent: daily habit formation through streaks, challenges, and smart AI notifications
- Accessible: 24/7 availability, no scheduling, no judgment
- Motivating: colorful progress indicators, achievement badges, interactive lesson cards, engaging animations

## Strategic Principles
- Replace expensive, schedule-bound human tutors with an always-available AI alternative
- Measurable progress is core to retention: every session produces visible scores and streaks
- Personalization is the key differentiator: one-size-fits-all apps fail speaking learners
- Notifications keep learners consistent; consistency is the #1 predictor of fluency gain
